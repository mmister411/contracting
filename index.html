About Us

Mister Ventures Incorporated (MVI) is a Service-Disabled Veteran Operated Small Business that specializes in Prime Contracting. We provide a wide range of services to help businesses acquire and complete government contracts.

Our Mission

Our mission is to serve as an information repository for learning the business of acquiring and completing government contracts. We aim to empower businesses with the knowledge and resources they need to succeed in the government contracting industry.

We are excited to announce that Mister Ventures Incorporated (MVI) will be officially launching in mid 2024. Stay tuned for updates and opportunities to collaborate with us.
